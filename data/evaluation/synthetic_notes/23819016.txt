Patient exhibited profound cachexia and protein-calorie malnutrition consistent with chronic alcohol use and underlying hepatic dysfunction. Findings consistent with wet beriberi (thiamine deficiency) and polyneuropathy were noted on examination. Alcoholic hepatitis without ascites documented on clinical and laboratory grounds. History of asthma remained uncomplicated during admission.
